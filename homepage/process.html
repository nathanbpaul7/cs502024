Catching Fish

February 13, 2024 by Lonnie

Catching fish requires strict adherence to a flexible routine.

  • Find a space where you can embark on sonic explorations
  • Bring the people you love into that space
  • Lower the bar for what constitutes art, music, poetry, and let it flow, together
  • Listen to one another. Find ways to support or reflect the intention you feel from your fam
  • Be sure to let the river flow through a collector field (of your choice, can be analog or digital)
  • Hire a licensed fish gutter, or help kinfolk gain gutter skills, share your knowledge of cleaning, smoking, and ultimately enjoying the fish.

These steps should get you well on your way to building your own collection of smoked fish, maybe even your own fish cave!
