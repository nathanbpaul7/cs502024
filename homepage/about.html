Early Life

Lonnie was born on the east coast, and don't you forget it.

Formal

From a young age, Lonnie was conscripted into a Suzuki platoon, complete with micromanaged practice sessions. From here a technical facility with keyboards, notation, rhythm and performance was developed, alongside crucial ear training.

Friendly

After a rigorous program of mostly classical musical skill-building, Lonnie progressed to some self-guided exploration of instruments like mandolin, drums, guitar, and bass. Alongside musical compatriots in his local town, he became a foundational member of a thriving suburban teen music scene.

A Career in Music

After graduating from a liberal arts college, Lonnie joined multiple musical groups, toured the United States and Canada, and honed his chops as a rhythm kit bit master.
